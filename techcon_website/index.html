The 2024 Tech Conference held in Midrand, South Africa, was a pivotal event that united industry leaders,
innovators, and tech enthusiasts to explore the latest advancements in technology. Hosted at the Gallagher
Convention Centre, the conference featured keynote speeches from top executives at major tech firms,
hands-on workshops, and panel discussions on emerging trends. Attendees had the opportunity to network, share insights, and experience cutting-edge products in the interactive expo hall, making it a landmark event for the future of technology in Africa.
